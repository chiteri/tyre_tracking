Tyre transfers

This system allows you to transfer tyres from one axel to the other e.g from the front to the trailer or from one trailer axel to another. You can also move it from one truck to any other truck without losing its history.

View details »

Trucks History

The system has an option of writing off any burst tyres but never losing its history. You are able to generate an accurate report on all burst tyres at any given time. Additionally, all tyres purchased within a given duration is shown giving all the details of the wheel (serial number, make, truck fitted, etc)

View details »

Report continuity

Tyre tracking has a continuity in reporting. The summaries and histories of the tyres as from the date of purchase, after retreading and any transfers, be it within the same truck or any other truck is never lost.
